Synopsis

Advik is a Rakshasa.

Not the monster from bedtime stories. Not the villain people expect.

A young man hiding an ancient nature while trying to live an ordinary life.

But when corruption, cruelty, and injustice threaten those he loves, his restraint is tested.

How long can a Rakshasa remain merciful?

Main Characters

Advik

A Rakshasa living among humans. Protective, intelligent, and far more dangerous than he appears.

Chetana

Software engineer. Curious, courageous, and determined to understand the truth.

Jagannath

Scholar of the Puranas and Chetana's father. A man who understands that character matters more than birth.

Genres & Themes

Genres: Urban Fantasy, Mythological Fiction, Romance, Supernatural Drama

Themes: Identity, Love, Duty, Justice, Power, Redemption, Faith
